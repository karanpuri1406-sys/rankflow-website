# RankAI — AI-Powered SEO Agency (Chandigarh)

## What This Is

An AI-powered SEO agency based in Chandigarh that helps local businesses, Indian SMEs, and international clients grow organic traffic faster and cheaper than traditional agencies. The agency differentiates through AI-driven workflows, modern offerings (GEO, programmatic SEO, video SEO, automation), and transparent results-focused delivery — built by a 2-5 person team transitioning from established freelance experience.

## Core Value

Clients get SEO results faster and at better ROI by leveraging AI tools and automation that most agencies haven't adopted yet.
