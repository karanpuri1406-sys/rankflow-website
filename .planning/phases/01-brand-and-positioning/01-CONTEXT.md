<decisions>
## Implementation Decisions

### Agency Name Style
- One-word name preferred (clean domain, easy to say and remember)
- Outcome-focused style — name signals results, not technology (e.g., RankFast, GrowthSignal, SearchEdge)
- "AI" should NOT appear literally in the name — AI positioning carried by brand story and messaging
- Acceptable domain extensions: .com or .co (both are credible for agencies)
- Name must have available .com or .co domain — check availability before finalizing

### Positioning Angle
- Core promise: **Speed** — "Get results faster than traditional agencies" (primary hook)
- Secondary differentiator: **Transparency** — clients can always see what's working, real data not vanity metrics
- Tagline tone: Technical and precise — "AI-augmented SEO for SaaS growth" style (speaks to technical founders)
- Target audience: Keep broad — lead with SaaS/tech startups but don't explicitly exclude local businesses (allows early local clients for case studies while building SaaS pipeline)
- Core frustration solved: Clients who waited 6+ months and got nothing; clients who got vanity reports with no real data

### Service Tier Definitions

**Starter — ₹40-60K/month:**
- Monthly technical SEO audit (crawl, Core Web Vitals, issue prioritization)
- On-page optimization: 5 pages/month
- Keyword research report (monthly opportunities + ranking targets)
- Monthly performance report (GA4 + GSC: traffic, rankings, wins, next steps)

**Growth — ₹80-150K/month (everything in Starter, plus):**
- On-page optimization: 10 pages/month (scaled)
- 10 SEO blog articles/month (AI-assisted, human-reviewed)
- Link building: 5-10 quality links/month
- Local SEO optimization (GBP management, citations, map pack)
- Competitor gap analysis (monthly: what competitors rank for that client doesn't)

**AI-Powered Premium — ₹150-300K/month (everything in Growth, plus):**
- On-page optimization: 15 pages/month (scaled)
- 15 SEO blog articles/month (AI pipeline for technical/SaaS content)
- GEO optimization (Google AI Overviews, ChatGPT Search, Perplexity citations)
- Dedicated account manager (weekly calls, priority response, proactive strategy)
- CRO + conversion tracking setup (align SEO traffic with conversion goals, heatmaps, funnel analysis)

**One-Time Audit (Lead Magnet):**
- Free mini-audit as entry point (5-point check: technical health, keyword gaps, on-page score, speed, competitors)
- Goal: Convert free audit → paid full audit → retainer
- No paid standalone audit product in v1 — audit is part of lead gen funnel

### Claude's Discretion
- Exact logo concept / visual execution (founder said minimalist style; planner can select specific direction)
- Color palette specific hex values (minimalist palette direction is set; specifics left to designer judgment)
- Typography selection (clean, technical sans-serif appropriate for SaaS-adjacent agency)
- Name candidates to generate and test for domain availability

</decisions>
