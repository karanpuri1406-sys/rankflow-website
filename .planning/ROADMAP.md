## Phase Details

### Phase 1: Brand & Positioning

**Goal:** Agency is positioned, priced, and packaged so first clients know exactly what to expect and at what cost.

**Depends on:** None (first phase)

**Requirements:** BRAND-01, BRAND-02, BRAND-03, BRAND-04, SVC-01, SVC-02, SVC-03, SVC-04, SVC-05

**Success Criteria** (what must be TRUE when phase completes):
1. Agency has a registered name with available .com domain (BRAND-01 achieved)
2. Positioning statement written and tested with 3-5 target SaaS prospects for clarity
3. Three service tiers defined with exact deliverables, scope, and pricing visible to prospects
4. Visual identity (logo concept, color palette, typography) consistent across all brand touchpoints
5. Agency tagline is memorable, mentions AI positioning, and differentiates from generic SEO agencies

**Plans:** TBD

### Phase 2: Legal & Operations Framework

**Goal:** Contracts and onboarding are bulletproof so client expectations are clear, disputes are prevented, and payment flows predictably.

**Depends on:** Phase 1 (must know service offerings before documenting SOPs)

**Requirements:** OPS-01, OPS-02, OPS-03, OPS-04, OPS-05

**Success Criteria** (what must be TRUE when phase completes):
1. Client intake form captures all critical information (business, goals, current SEO state, website access, budget)
2. Service agreement template covers scope, timeline, payment terms, IP ownership, and NDA (prevents disputes)
3. Onboarding email sequence is written and tested (welcome → kickoff → first week check-in)
4. Kickoff call agenda template ensures consistent first interaction (goals, timeline, success metrics)
5. Monthly reporting template shows traffic, rankings, wins, and next steps in client-friendly format

**Plans:** TBD

### Phase 3: Website Infrastructure

**Goal:** Agency has a live, professional, mobile-responsive website that serves as credibility anchor and homepage for all lead gen channels.

**Depends on:** Phase 1 (positioning must exist to write copy), Phase 2 (contact form connects to ops)

**Requirements:** WEB-01, WEB-02, WEB-08, WEB-09

**Success Criteria** (what must be TRUE when phase completes):
1. Next.js site deployed on Vercel and accessible at agency domain
2. Homepage has hero section, value proposition, 3-tier services overview, and clear CTA ("Get Free Audit")
3. Services page displays all three packages with pricing, deliverables, and "Get Started" buttons
4. Website is mobile-responsive and passes Google Core Web Vitals (LCP, FID, CLS)
5. Website has SEO foundation (meta tags, Open Graph, robots.txt, XML sitemap, schema markup)

**Plans:** TBD

### Phase 4: Lead Capture & Contact

**Goal:** Visitors can easily request audits and contact agency, capturing names and emails for lead gen follow-up.

**Depends on:** Phase 3 (website must exist before adding forms)

**Requirements:** WEB-06, WEB-10

**Success Criteria** (what must be TRUE when phase completes):
1. Contact page is live with form for inquiries (name, email, website, message)
2. Lead magnet page (Free SEO Audit) is live and standalone (shareable, trackable)
3. Lead magnet captures email in exchange for free audit offer (no paywall)
4. Forms integrate with CRM (Zoho) so leads automatically enter sales pipeline
5. Form confirmations trigger first nurture email (welcome, set expectation for audit timeline)

**Plans:** TBD

### Phase 5: Content & SEO Setup

**Goal:** Website is positioned as SEO-informed agency with owned content demonstrating expertise and ranking for target keywords.

**Depends on:** Phase 3 (website structure exists)

**Requirements:** WEB-05, WEB-03, WEB-04

**Success Criteria** (what must be TRUE when phase completes):
1. Blog section with CMS (Sanity free tier) is live and functional for publishing articles
2. About page tells founder story, explains SEO expertise, and positions AI-powered approach
3. Services page details are expanded with 3-tier packages, pricing, and differentiators visible
4. Website is optimized for target keywords ("SEO agency SaaS", "SEO agency Chandigarh", "AI-powered SEO")
5. SEO strategy document created (target keyword list, content calendar, publishing cadence)

### Phase 6: Case Studies & Social Proof

**Goal:** Website demonstrates results with real before/after case studies so prospects believe agency delivers.

**Depends on:** Phase 7 (case studies need real client work or internal pilot)

**Requirements:** WEB-07

**Success Criteria** (what must be TRUE when phase completes):
1. Case studies page structure is built with template for: Client name, challenge, solution, results (traffic/rankings/leads)
2. At least 1 published case study shows real before/after metrics (internal pilot or first client)
3. Case study includes: Methodology, timeline, specific tactics used, KPIs achieved, client quote
4. Social proof elements visible on homepage (client logo grid, testimonial snippet, or success statistic)
5. Case study is formatted for SEO and shared on LinkedIn/referral channels for lead gen

**Plans:** TBD

### Phase 7: AI & Content Workflow

**Goal:** Content production pipeline is defined, secure, and repeatable so agency can deliver AI-augmented content without quality risks.

**Depends on:** Phase 1 (know service packages), Phase 2 (contracts clarify content ownership)

**Requirements:** AI-01, AI-02, AI-03

**Success Criteria** (what must be TRUE when phase completes):
1. Claude API is integrated into workflow (outline generation, draft creation, optimization checks)
2. SEO toolstack (Semrush free tier or equivalent, Google Search Console, Looker Studio) is set up and team has access
3. Content QA checklist is documented (8-point review: plagiarism check, AI detection, fact verification, E-E-A-T, SEO compliance, readability, tone, formatting)
4. Full content workflow documented: Outline (Claude) → Draft (human) → AI optimization check → SEO review → Publish
5. Reporting dashboard (Looker Studio) shows website health, GA4 tracking, keyword rankings, lead capture metrics

**Plans:** TBD

### Phase 8: Lead Generation Activation

**Goal:** Agency is actively generating qualified leads through owned SEO, LinkedIn, and inbound channels.

**Depends on:** Phase 3 (website must rank for keywords), Phase 4 (contact form must work), Phase 7 (toolstack ready to track leads)

**Requirements:** LEAD-01, LEAD-02, LEAD-03, LEAD-04

**Success Criteria** (what must be TRUE when phase completes):
1. Agency website is optimized for target keywords and beginning to rank for "SEO agency SaaS" / "SEO agency Chandigarh"
2. LinkedIn agency page is created with company branding, company description, and call-to-action
3. Founder LinkedIn profile is optimized with SEO agency positioning, company link, and headline targeting decision-makers
4. Outreach message templates are written and tested (4-5 variations for cold LinkedIn outreach to SaaS founders)
5. Lead tracking is enabled: website visits → audit requests → CRM pipeline → outreach follow-up cadence established

## Notes

- **Phase 1 is a gate:** Positioning, pricing, and service definitions must be locked before any delivery or website work. Research flags this as critical pitfall #2 and #3.
- **Phase 2 prevents disputes:** Contracts, intake forms, and onboarding SOPs prevent the "undefined expectations" refund disasters.
- **Phases 3-5 are website delivery:** Split into infrastructure (3), lead capture (4), and content (5) for parallel execution and clear success criteria.
- **Phase 6 depends on Phase 7:** Case studies require real work (client or internal pilot) to be credible.
- **Phase 7 is workflow foundation:** AI content QA checklist and reporting dashboard prevent low-quality AI content penalties.
- **Phase 8 launches growth:** Once website is live, SEO-optimized, and lead capture works, begin lead gen outreach and track results.
- **Fine granularity (8 phases) matches scope:** Each phase is focused, independent, and tests one critical capability.
