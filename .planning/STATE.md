## Critical Pitfalls to Prevent

From research, these phase-gated risks must be addressed:

| Pitfall | Risk | Phase Gate | Mitigation |
|---------|------|-----------|-----------|
| Wrong positioning / underpricing | Race to bottom; can't scale | Phase 1 | Lock defensible positioning + 3-tier pricing before site launch |
| Scope creep | Margins evaporate | Phase 1 | Define service boundaries in contract (no PPC, social, email) |
| AI content without QA = penalties | Google penalties on clients | Phase 7 | Implement 8-point QA checklist; plagiarism + AI detection tools |
| No clear contract | Refund disputes | Phase 2 | Template SOW + service agreement prevents verbal misalignment |
| Capacity overload | Poor delivery, churn | Phase 8 | Set client limit (5-8 solo max) before heavy outreach |
| No own SEO / case studies | "Where are your results?" | Phase 6 | Publish 1 case study before outbound sales |
| Payment delays | Cash flow collapse | Phase 2 | 50% upfront rule in contract + invoicing reminders |
